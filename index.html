☕ Coffee Varieties: A Guide ☕

Coffee is a beloved beverage with many forms. From the strong espresso to the milky latte, there's a type for every preference.

Espresso

A strong, concentrated shot of coffee, serving as the base for many other drinks.

Latte

Made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small layer of foamed milk.

Cappuccino

Espresso with a rich, thick layer of steamed milk foam on top.
